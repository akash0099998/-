## 📌 About Goat Bot V2

**Goat Bot V2** is a powerful Facebook Messenger automation bot designed for:

- **Group Management** – Keep your groups organized and safe.
- **Auto Reply System** – Intelligent responses to messages.
- **Entertainment Commands** – Fun and interactive commands.
- **Custom Automation** – Tailor it to your needs with ease.

> ⚠️ This repository is a **modified educational fork** maintained by **Mohammad Akash**. All core credit goes to the original developer.
